📊 YOUR FORMULAS (all implemented):

  Net Cost:
    Fixed Grant:     Installation_Cost - Grant
    Percentage:      Installation_Cost * (1 - Percentage)

  Annual Savings:
    Monthly_Bill * 12 * Efficiency_Factor

  Payback Period:
    Net_Cost / Annual_Savings

  10-Year Scenarios:
    Status Quo:      Bill * 12 * 1.05^year (for 10 years)
    Heat Pump:       Net_Cost + (Reduced_Bill * 12 * 1.05^year)
    Daily Loss:      (Status Quo - Heat Pump) / 3650

  Results include year-by-year breakdown table!
